Privacy Policy

Your data is yours — we protect it with care. Last updated: June 2026.

1. Information We Collect

At Aurexon, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, shipping and billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely by our partners)
  • IP address, browser type, and device information for analytics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process orders and deliver your purchases
  • To send order confirmations, updates, and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • To improve our website, products, and customer experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud
We never sell your personal data. Your trust is our priority.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Third-party services (like analytics) may also use cookies.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We share your information only with trusted partners who help us operate our business — such as shipping carriers, payment processors, and analytics providers. They are bound by confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for other purposes.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Correction: update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: request that we delete your data
  • Opt-out: unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@Aurexon.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Order records are kept for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Aurexon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or a notice on our website. The effective date will be clearly indicated.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our Privacy Team:
